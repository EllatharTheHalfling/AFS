ABOUT US

We're a small team that has big dreams. With a passion for games, both in playing and the desire to create, we hope to bring stories that fill people with the same excitement, passion, and emotion that we pour in. Some of our projects might seem ambitious at first, however we have the drive to see each and every project through.

OUR GAMES

At Arctic Flare Studios our goal is always the story. It is our belief that all other aspects of a game are there to showcase the stories that the players experience. Short, or long, big, or small; every story we tell has an end, sometimes that ending is sad, sometimes it's happy, but no matter what we strive for it to be the best ending that we can create.

JOB OPENINGS

The projects we aim to complete are not small, not by any means, and we need people, but more importantly we want people that will fit. Do you have a passion for games? Do you want to create them? Are you open to working on games that vary greatly in thematic elements? Look at what we're needing, see if that's you. If it isn't then by all means drop us an email and we'll look over what you have to offer.


CONCEPT ILLUSTRATOR

This is huge. Before models can be made, before stages can be built, we need someone that can turn our visions into something people can see.

CONCEPT ILLUSTRATOR

Who we want:

We are ultimately looking for someone who isn't afraid to branch out in style, plain and simple. Because we intend to cover so many different themes and styles we need people just as diverse as our games.


REQUIREMENTS:

  • Open minded about art direction.

  • Willing to ask questions.

  • self-motivated. We can't always check in on you.

  • Organized and good with communication.

  • Passion and pride in your work.


HOW TO APPLY:

Drop us a line, send us your portfolio. We're currently working on a game based around 80s retrofuturism. We won't turn you down if it's not there, but we will ask and give you time to come up with an example. Either way, go ahead and email us, we'll be happy to hear from you. jobs@arcticflarestudios
